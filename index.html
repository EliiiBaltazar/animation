What is Sonny Angel?

Sonny Angel is a cute boy angel who likes wearing all sorts of headgear. He doesn’t talk, but Sonny Angel makes you happy and makes your daily life bit more delightful. As your tiny friend, Sonny Angel is always by your side, to protect you, give you comfort, and make you smile… that is Sonny Angel’s mission.
